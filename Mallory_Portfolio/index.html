About Me

Up until 2020 hit, I was on track to stay in the nonprofit world and work in social media. As the COVID-19 pandemic has continued to shift my, and many people’s, priorities, I began to look at other career paths. Both my dad and brother are software developers and have always encouraged me to look into it.

After spending a few months researching and making sure it was the right move for me, I enrolled in The Tech Academy’s Software Developer Bootcamp. And thus began my software development journey and this portfolio!

I am excited to bring my graphic design, web development, and social media skills to this new career and I cannot wait to see the opportunities it brings. When I am not behind the computer for this course or my social media freelance job, you can find me with a latte in hand, walking my rescue dog Waffle in Central Park!
